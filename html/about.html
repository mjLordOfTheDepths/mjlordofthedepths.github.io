Who Am I ?

My name is M.J. Moore. I am a third year computer science student at TU Dublin.
I am a big fan of programming and technology in general, and am particularly interested in programming, networking, and system administration.
I am a sys admin for my university's computer science society, CS++.

I have experience with a variety of programming languages, including but not limited to: Python, C, C++, Java, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Rust, Bash, and SQL.
I am an avid user of Unix based operating systems, having used Linux as my primary kernel since 2020, and recently using FreeBSD as my daily driver.

Outside of programming, I am a fan of SCUBA - I am a PADI certified Advanced Open Water Diver and Deep Diver, and I enjoy underwater photography.
I also enjoy gaming, reading, and watching films.
